Questions

Before you book your trial class

Are classes online or in person?

Every class is simulcast live — attend in person at our Mississauga campus, or join the same session via Zoom from anywhere in Canada or overseas.

Can I try a class first?

Yes — we offer a free trial class for MCCQE1 or NAC‑OSCE, with no obligation to enrol afterward.

Do I need to start on a set date?

No. Topics rotate continuously, so you can join at any point and keep attending until your exam date.

Is the MCCQE1 course up to date?

Yes. Since April 2025 the Clinical Decision Making component is no longer part of the exam, and our materials reflect the current format.
